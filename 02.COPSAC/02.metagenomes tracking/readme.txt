00. code.sh
Shell script for the strain-tracking analysis pipeline, including preprocessing and quality control of metagenomic sequencing data, preparation of clean reads, and downstream strain-tracking analyses.

01. strain_tracking_results.xlsx
Results of the strain-tracking analysis based on metagenomic sequencing data, including tracking outcomes for samples collected at the 1-month and 1-year follow-up time points.

02. exposures_tests.R
R script used to assess associations between environmental exposure variables and the presence of the top 10 most prevalent strains identified in the study.
